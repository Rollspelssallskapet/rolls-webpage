About Us

With Passion for Roleplaying games in Malmö!

We are a non-profit association consisting of enthusiastic people who all love to play roleplaying games.

The goal of the association is to produce wonderful and epic gaming opportunities, consisting of players and Game Masters, in a safe and stimulating environment.

Everyone are welcome to visit us and try out the roleplaying experience for themselves on our schedualed events.

On these occasions, you can talk to members, discuss the game, membership, become a member, participate in games and workshops or just watch others play.

Become a Member!

To create our esteemed assembly, a platform is needed for us to stand on - the Association!

At Rollspelssällskapet, everyone is welcome, young and old to enjoy all that a gaming opportunity can offer!

During our activities, the association makes sure there is room for everyone to participate, tea or coffee to enjoy during the game, as well as provide everything that you may need to be able to experience the game to the fullest!

The association's vision is also to provide a safe and reinforcing environment for aspiring Game Masters.

Do you feel that the next step for you is to start lead games, or are you curious about what it actually means to lead a game, we are here to support, help and answer all the questions we can!

You can participate twice in our open activities to try things out before becoming a member.

Benefits of being a member

  • Participate in our activities that we organize at Spelens Hus, such as game sessions and workshops.
  • Enjoy our freshly brewed coffee and tea.
  • Use our roleplaying game resources such as mats and miniatures.
  • Access to books and reference materials.
  • Participate in and influence the development of the association.
  • Help get started and develop your roleplaying skills.
  • Discount at Playoteket. (On presentation of membership card)
  • Have real fun at least once a week.

To become a member:

  • Agree to follow our statutes (in Swedish) and our values.
  • Pay our annual membership fee. 40 SEK for those younger than 26 or older than 65 years. 100 SEK for those who are between 26-65 years old. You apply through SVEROKs tool ebas (in Swedish).
    Further information on how to pay the membership fee can be found by clicking the button below.
    • Apply to become a member
  • If you have already been a member and want to re-register for the new membership year, you can use the same button.

Values

Rollspelssällskapets values ​​are intended to ensure an sustainable and inclusive setting where all members feel accepted and welcome by allowing them to express themselves and interact in a safe and supportive environment.

The following points clarify the basic rules that apply for the association:

  • All members of the association are welcome and must be treated equally regardless of gender, transgender identity or expression, ethnic or political affiliation, religion or other belief, functional variation and sexual orientation. In some of the association's activities one must have reached the age of 18, with the consent of the guardian one can participate from the age of 15.
  • The association's platform or resources may not be used for individual, political, economically or other purposes.
  • All members must respect each other's views and strive to resolve conflicts and disagreements through dialogue between the parties.
  • The association shall work for a easy and stress-free relation. Members shall in a respectful way help each other as much as possible and be tolerant of new players and aspiring GMs.
  • If an SL is unsatisfactory for you as a player, you are encouraged, with support from the association, to lead the game yourself.
  • Members are expected to arrive on time for the association's activities.

The following points clarify the basic rules that apply to activities:

  • GM may add additional rules provided that these do not violate values ​​and statutes.
  • Adding sexual themes such as rape, sexual violence, sexual abuse of power and other negative sexual themes should be avoided and may lead to exclusion from the association. Portraying individuals based on their sexuality should be done with respect and dignity.
  • Including social issues such as addiction, poverty, violence in close relationships, and more, in the game should be done with an awareness and respect.
  • Bullying or harassment between players or GM is not tolerated. This also applies during roleplaying. Everyone has the right to express his character without being met with ridicule or being silenced by GM or other players.
  • GM shall strive to distribute space fairly for players around the table by allowing all players to speak and participate and that players shall understand that it is a limited social space.
  • In case of rule disputes, GM has the last word and the right to define how rules are to be interpreted, GM should be consistent with decisions. GM should respect the table's overall will and opinion.
  • Actively opposing GM can be a basis for GM excluding a member from continued roleplay.
  • Players must refrain from disappearing into discussions regarding non-campaign related topics during the playing time.
  • Players should strive to learn the basic rules of the roleplaying game as well as the player's own character traits.
